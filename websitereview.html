ESPN

I also like that they have little pictures and logos next to the categories. ESPN uses a lot of red, white, and black for their main colors. I believe that picked colors that are very "sporty" colors or in other words colors that are very popular for uniforms in the different sports leagues. They curve the edges of the photos and videos. Their text is very clean looking and is very easy to see. Overall I think that ESPN is a classic website that is widely used and is very popular across the world. I believe that many people rely on this site for scores and news and much more.
